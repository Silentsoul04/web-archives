JVN#73258608
CruiseWorks and Minna De Office vulnerable in access restrictions

Overview

CruiseWorks and Minna De Office are groupware. They contain a vulnerability that the user's access restriction is not properly set.

Products Affected

  • CruiseWorks 1.09e and earlier
  • Minna De Office version 1.xx and 2.xx
For more information, refer to the vendors' websites.

Description

Impact

An user with a standard privilege who logs into CruiseWorks or Minna De Office could possibly change the system configurations or information registered.
